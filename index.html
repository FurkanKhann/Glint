User Manual: How to use Glint

1

Summon the Assistant

Glint lives in the background. Simply run the app, and whenever you need it, click its icon or use the global shortcut to bring the search bar to the front of your screen.

2

Ask or Command

Type anything into the search bar. You can ask general questions or give it commands (e.g., "Write a polite email to my boss"). Press Enter to submit.

3

Using Context

Highlight text in ANY app (browser, Word, Slack) before triggering Glint. The app will capture your selected text as context. You can then use the quick actions like "Fix Grammar" or "Make Formal".

4

Customization

Click the gear icon in the bottom right corner. You can change the background color, transparency, and even give the AI a custom persona instruction (e.g. "Act as a software engineer").

OpenRouter API Key Setup

To use cloud AI models freely and extend your 50 request quota, you can plug in your own OpenRouter API key.

  1. Go to OpenRouter.ai and sign up for a free account.
  2. Navigate to the Keys section in your OpenRouter dashboard.
  3. Click Create Key and copy the generated key (it starts with sk-or-v1-).
  4. Open Glint and click the Settings (Gear icon) in the bottom right corner.
  5. Paste your key under the OpenRouter Free Key section and click Save Settings.
